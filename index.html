Tax Consultant Services

Esa Anugerah provides tax consulting services to help clients achieve a good understanding of taxation and create effective tax management

Our tax services include:

  • Monthly Tax Compliance Assistance
  • We provide monthly tax consultation services for the calculation, payment, and monthly reporting of all tax SPT Masa PPh Pasal 21, PPh Pasal 22, PPh Pasal 23, PPh Pasal 24, PPh Pasal 25, PPh Pasal 4 (2), PPh Pasal 15, PPh Pasal 26, and SPT Masa PPN and PPnBM.
  • Annual Tax Compliance Assistance
  • We provide consultation services for the calculation, payment, and reporting of Annual SPT PPh Pasal 29 for Individuals and Entities.
  • Tax Audit Assistance
  • We act as a Power of Attorney to represent clients who are being examined/audited by tax auditors. Our services include:
    • 1. Providing assistance to clients during tax audits
    • 2. Submitting books/records/accounting documents to tax auditors
    • 3. Representing clients in meetings with tax auditors to provide requested explanations/information
    • 4. Accompanying clients in receiving the SPHP (Tax Audit Result) from the Examiner
    • 5. Accompanying the final audit discussion (closing) with the Examiner
    • 6. Receiving the legal product of the Audit, namely the SKP (Tax Assessment Letter)
  • Tax Compliance Review
  • We can help review your tax compliance reports to ensure that they comply with applicable tax regulations. Our services include identifying findings, providing advice, and recommendations to ensure that your tax compliance meets the applicable tax regulations.
  • Tax Planning
  • We assist clients in applying appropriate tax planning for effective tax payments while maintaining client cash flow. Our tax planning services are based on applicable tax regulations.
  • Tax Administration Assistance
  • We provide tax administration services such as:
    • 1. Handling NPWP (Taxpayer Identification Number)
    • 2. PKP (Taxable Entrepreneur)
    • 3. SKB (Certificate of Exemption)
    • 4. Representing clients in responding to summons from the Tax Office in the form of Reminders, Clarifications, Equalizations, and conducting Counseling Minutes at the Tax Service Office
    • 5. Other tax administration services
  • Tax Information System Development
  • We provide tax information system development services to help clients organize their bookkeeping in a neat and orderly manner that can be equalized with SPT reporting, minimizing errors in bookkeeping as supporting evidence for SPT reports, and making clients more prepared for tax audits.

Audit Support: Your Reliable Partner for a Smooth Audit Process

Esa Anugerah understands that the audit process can be complex and time-consuming. That's why we offer Audit Support services to help your company undergo internal and external audits smoothly and efficiently.

What do we offer?

  • Financial Statement Preparation: Our experienced team can assist you in preparing accurate financial statements that comply with applicable accounting standards, such as SAK (Indonesian Financial Accounting Standards), SAK ETAP (Indonesian Accounting Standards for Entities Without Public Accountability), and IFRS (International Financial Accounting Standards)
  • Liaison and Support: We act as a liaison between your company and the auditors, facilitating smooth communication and coordination throughout the audit process.
  • Information and Data Preparation: Our team is ready to assist you in collecting and preparing the information and data required by the auditors in a complete and timely manner.
  • Accounting Standards Expertise: We have in-depth knowledge and understanding of accounting standards applicable in Indonesia and internationally.
